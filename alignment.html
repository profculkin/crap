Alignment

Every element should have some visual connection with another element on the page. Notice how in the top example, the lines of text are aligned either to the left edge or right edge. Decorative elements, like the lines, are also oriented to the placement of the text, as is the image. In the lower example, nothing is aligned to anything else, it looks chaotic.
