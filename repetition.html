Repetition

Repetition means repeating elements of your design to create consistency in the look of your page. The top example creates elements using a limited color palette and repetition of the same size and shape element. In the lower example, the elements are all different sizes, colors and orientations.

Repetition does not have to literally mean a grid, it could mean repetition of a color, typeface, line, something subtler.
